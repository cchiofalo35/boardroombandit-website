Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 March 2026

Boardroom Bandit ("the App") is developed and operated by Zorro Studios Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Data We Collect

Boardroom Bandit stores all game data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • Game progress, achievements, and settings are stored only on your device using local storage
  • No account creation or sign-in is required
  • No data is sent to our servers or any third parties
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising tools are used
  • No cookies are used

2. In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional in-app purchases. All purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store infrastructure. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

3. Game Data

The App stores game-related data including your company name, sector holdings, staff, assets, achievements, and prestige level. This data is:

  • Stored exclusively on your device
  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Deletable by resetting the game or uninstalling the App

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. The only third-party service involved is Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app purchases.

5. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, all associated data is permanently removed. We do not retain any copies of your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Boardroom Bandit does not contain content unsuitable for general audiences, however it is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times. You can:

  • View all game data within the App
  • Reset your game progress at any time
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the App

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
